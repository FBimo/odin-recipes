Tutty Fruity Ice Jelly

Description

One of the most popular variants is the tutty fruity ice jelly recipe,

which combines the freshness and fun of fruit with the pleasure of creamy jelly.

Tutty fruity ice jelly originates from Southeast Asian cultures, especially in

countries such as Indonesia, Malaysia and Thailand. Usually served as a

dessert or refreshing drink, tutty fruity ice jelly has become an indispensable

part of festival and celebratory dishes in the area.

Main Ingredients

  • 2 packs of Strawberry Nutrijel

  • 2 packs of grass jelly Nutrijel

  • 1 pack of melon Nutrijel

  • 1 pack of grapes Nutrijel

  • 1 mango, cut into small pieces

  • 1 orange, cut into small pieces

  • Strawberry jam to taste

  • 1 can of evaporated milk

  • 1 liter of UHT milk

  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Cook all Nutrijel ingredients according to package directions. Then grate

    nutrijel grass jelly, set aside. Dice all the nutrijels

  2. Mix UHT milk, evaporated milk and sweetened condensed milk. Stir well and taste test

  3. Grease the bottom of the glass with strawberry jam. Add jelly and flush

    with milk mixture

  4. Lastly, add grated grass jelly and pieces of mango and oranges, then serve cold.
