Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of the most famous cuisine in the world. This Italian food

is mostly made from flat pasta noodles, cheeses, beef, and bolognese sauce.

The taste is undoubtedly delicious and appetizing. Very suitable for those who

like Western cuisine. Mostly, this dish is baked in the oven. However, if you

don't have any of it you can still make it by steaming it in steamer. It is very

practical and economical because you don't have to buy an oven first.

Main Ingredients

  • 4 sheets of lasagna sheets

  • 1 tsp cooking oil

  • 1/2 tsp salt

  • Grated cheddar cheese

  • Quick Melt Cheese, grated

  • Oregano

  • Aluminum foil cups

Cheese Sauce Ingredients

  • 500 mL UHT full cream milk

  • 2 tbsp flour

  • 1/2 tsp salt

  • 2 tbsp butter/margarine

  • 2 tbsp cheddar cheese

  • 1 tbsp Quick Melt Cheese

  • 2 tbsp cream cheese

  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg

Meat Sauce Ingredients

  • 100 grams of ground beef

  • 2 small onions

  • 1 pack of instant bolognaise sauce

  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg

  • 1/2 tsp ground pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare a pan, pour water, bring to a boil, add lasagna, boil with oil and

    salt until al dente (not too hard or too soft)

  2. Melt the butter/margarine. Add the flour, stir until dissolved, pour in the

    milk, stir again and make sure there are no lumps

  3. Add all the ingredients for the cheese sauce, add the pepper and ground

    nutmeg, stir until thickened. Add the oregano, stir briefly, turn off the heat,

    and set aside

  4. Prepare a frying pan, pour a little oil, turn on the fire, add the meat sauce,

    saut√© with the onions until wilted. Add ground beef to it, stir and cook until

    the color changes

  5. Pour in instant bolognaise sauce, add pepper and ground nutmeg, cook

    until the sauce bubbles. Taste whether the taste is to your taste or not. If

    so, then turn off the heat and set aside

  6. Prepare the boiler, heat it, spread the aluminum foil cup with butter. Put

    the lasagna sheet, cheese sauce and meat sauce in the cup. Repeat until

    the cup is full (last layer)

  7. Sprinkle grated cheddar cheese, quick melt cheese and oregano on top

    Steam until the cheese melts

  8. If so, then remove and serve. Lasagna without oven ready to eat
