Lumpia Beef Lumer

Description

This snack is widely sold in various places.

However, those who sell it with so much stuffing

is not too much even though it is sold at a fairly

higher price than the usual. This snack has a taste

almost exactly the same as chicken meat, it's just

that this beef slice have very soft texture so that

you'll feel the delicious taste of the meat at the

the middle and end of your snack time.

Main Ingredients

  • Palm oil

  • 4 beefs burger patties

  • 20 fresh spring roll skins

  • 200 grams of curly lettuce leaves, coarsely sliced

  • 200 grams of cooked macaroni

  • 200 mL barbeque sauce

  • 4 sheets of cheddar slice cheese

  • 4 fried eggs

  • 200 mL of mayonnaise

  • Chili sauce

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of cooking oil in a flat pan, grill the beef patties until

    they are browned. Lift, set aside

  2. Arrange 5 pieces of spring roll skin on the kitchen table, put ¼ part of the

    lettuce and ¼ part of the macaroni

  3. Give enough BBQ sauce, put 1 piece of beef patty on it. Give a piece of

    cheese, fried eggs, quite a lot of mayonnaise and chili sauce according to

    taste

  4. Fold the spring roll skin into a square shape until all the contents are covered.

  5. Heat 2 tablespoons of cooking oil in a pan, place the spring rolls with the folds down.

  6. Bake the spring rolls until the outer skin is crispy and browned. Lift

  7. Lumpia beef melt is ready to eat!
